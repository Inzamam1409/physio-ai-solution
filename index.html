What is the problem Physio-AI Solution is Working On?

Around 2.3% of Indian Population is suffering with some or the other kind of disability!
Rehab aid is a fundamental part treatment for such disabilities. Although AI is being used overseas as a tool to improve safety and efficacy of these aids, access to affordable assissted devices/rehab aids in limited in India, and Integration of AI in this field is completely Missing!

Solution We Are Working On!

Integration of AI in commonly used rehab aids such as walking sticks, Walker.

It will help the person Using it in performing the concerned activity with more precision and safety.

This integration will also collect real time data the patient which will be used in research.
